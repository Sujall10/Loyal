04// OUR STORY

Started as a local spot for refreshing sodas and classic paans soon evolved into a youth hotspot, blending heritage, flavor, and style under one vibrant roof.

Born in the heart of Vadodara, Thakur Pan & Soda began with a simple idea — to take the timeless charm of India’s favorite street indulgences and give them a modern, premium twist.

We believe every moment deserves good taste, cozy comfort, and a touch of luxury — all wrapped in a setting built to impress. Thakur Pan has become a go-to destination for those seeking a unique experience.
