02// SHORT STORY

Since 1994, in the vibrant streets of Akshar Chowk, Thakur Pan has been crafting moments that taste like memories.

In 1994, amidst the vibrant crossroads of Akshar Chowk, a humble idea took shape — to serve not just paan, but moments of flavor and connection. What began as a small family venture soon became a local legend, drawing people from every corner of Vadodara to experience something refreshingly different.

Founded by 3 brothers with a passion for authentic taste and heartfelt service, Thakur Pan & Soda wasn't just about satisfying cravings — it was about creating memories, one sip and smile at a time.

Over the years, the name Thakur Pan became synonymous with premium taste, youthful energy, and timeless charm. Today, it stands as more than just a Shop — it's a cultural icon, where tradition meets trend, and every flavor tells a story worth sharing.

Today, Thakur Pan & Soda continues to captivate Vadodara with its perfect blend of heritage and innovation. The passion that began in 1994 still thrives — reimagined for a new generation that craves both authenticity and trend.

Each creation, from signature paans to artisanal mocktails, carries the same spirit of artistry, connection, and celebration. Locals and travelers alike are drawn to its warm ambiance and premium feel — where the charm of tradition meets the pulse of modern culture.

Through changing times and generations, one thing remains unchanged — the warmth, the flavor, and the feeling that every visit is something to remember. At its heart, Thakur Pan & Soda will always be what it was meant to be — a place where stories begin, friendships deepen, and every sip is a celebration.
